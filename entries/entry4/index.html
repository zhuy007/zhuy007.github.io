September 30, 2023
@m1keraz

Upon the pavement, I did spy,
A poster on the ground, where it did lie.
Neglected, torn, and weather-worn,
A tale of something lost, forlorn.

Its colors once so vivid, bright,
Now faded, dimmed in the soft daylight.
A message meant to catch the eye,
Now unnoticed, left to slowly die.
